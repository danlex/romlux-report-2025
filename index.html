President's Message

Building Quietly, with Purpose

About RomLux

Romania-Luxemburg Business Forum Asbl was founded in 2007 with a clear purpose: to build a lasting, credible bridge between the Romanian and Luxembourg business communities.

Starting as a small network of professionals and entrepreneurs, RomLux earned its standing through consistency and substance. Over 18 years, it has grown into the accredited Romanian Chamber of Commerce in Luxembourg, recognised by both Romanian and Luxembourg institutions.

Today, RomLux brings together over 200 members across sectors — finance, technology, law, consulting, and culture — united by a shared belief that bilateral cooperation, done well, creates real and durable value.

With more than 160 events organised since its establishment and a growing international footprint, RomLux continues to operate as a working platform: facilitating dialogue, enabling partnerships, and representing Romanian talent and enterprise with clarity and credibility.

Romania-Luxemburg Business Forum Asbl was created from a simple conviction: meaningful progress happens when trust, competence, and shared purpose meet. As the accredited Romanian Chamber of Commerce in the Grand Duchy of Luxembourg, our role is to serve as a reliable bridge between Romania and Luxembourg — connecting people, ideas, and initiatives that generate long-term value. RomLux is not just a network — it is a working platform. We act as enablers, supporting concrete projects that strengthen bilateral economic, cultural, scientific and professional ties.
Razvan-Petru Radu
President, Romania-Luxemburg Business Forum

How We Operate

RomLux is a non-profit association (A.s.b.l.) registered in Luxembourg, governed by its members and run primarily by volunteers committed to the bilateral cause. We do not conduct business on behalf of our members. We create the conditions in which business can happen.

Our Pillars

What We Do

RomLux operates as a bilateral business platform, connector and facilitator, delivering value through six complementary pillars of action.

Business Connectivity & Matchmaking

  • Networking events, forums, roundtables and B2B meetings
  • Introductions to partners, providers and institutions
  • RomLux Connect — directory of Romanian-founded companies in Luxembourg

Market Access & Internationalisation

  • Market briefings, country seminars and trade missions
  • Support for Romanian companies entering Luxembourg and the EU
  • National pavilions and curated delegations at major tech events

Knowledge Sharing & Capacity Building

  • RomLux Knowledge Café and Romanian Connect
  • Entrepreneurs Network and sector working groups
  • Member-led sessions, speakers and peer learning formats

Institutional Liaison & Advocacy

  • Cooperation with chambers, ministries and innovation agencies
  • Advocacy for bilateral initiatives — incl. Luxair LU–Bucharest line
  • High-level meetings between officials and business leaders

Visibility, Promotion & Nation Branding

  • Romanian pavilions at Nexus, Luxembourg Internet Days, ARCH Summit
  • Promoting Romanian startups and scale-ups in Luxembourg
  • Media exposure, digital presence and market intelligence content

Cultural & Charitable Engagement

  • Romanian Stand at Bazar International since 2007
  • Charity concerts, cultural exhibitions and artistic events
  • €17,800 raised for charity in 2025 — record contribution

Looking Ahead

Strategic Outlook 2026

As RomLux approaches its twentieth year of activity, the association enters a new phase of maturity, consolidation and strategic ambition.

Deepening Bilateral Economic Cooperation

  • Targeted business delegations and sector missions
  • Structured B2B matchmaking formats
  • Focus: IT, cybersecurity, fintech, space, logistics, energy

Strengthening Romania's Innovation Position

  • National pavilions at Nexus2050, ICT Spring, ARCH Summit
  • Introductions to Luxembourg investors and accelerators
  • Promoting Romania as a competitive EU innovation partner

Expanding Knowledge Sharing

  • Country seminars with Luxembourg Chamber of Commerce
  • Expanding Knowledge Café and Entrepreneurs Network
  • Thematic working groups on priority sectors

Scaling RomLux Connect Platform

  • Expanding registered companies on connect.romlux.org
  • Increasing visibility in business communities
  • Integrating into events, delegations and business missions

Enhancing Institutional Cooperation & Advocacy

  • Structured business exchanges with Luxembourg Chamber of Commerce
  • Strengthening cooperation with embassies and bilateral chambers
  • Promoting connectivity projects — building on Luxair LU–Bucharest flight

Sustaining Cultural Diplomacy & Charitable Engagement

  • Romanian stand at Bazar International de Luxembourg
  • Charity concerts, Race for a Cause karting and fundraising initiatives
  • Cultural exhibitions, theatre performances and concerts promoting Romanian heritage

Growing & Engaging the RomLux Community

  • Attracting new corporate and individual members
  • Increasing peer interaction, collaboration and cross-sector dialogue
  • Remaining member-driven, guided by inclusiveness and long-term relationship building

RomLux enters 2026 with a solid foundation

In a European context marked by economic transformation, technological acceleration and geopolitical uncertainty, the need for trusted bilateral platforms has never been greater.

By combining business connectivity, innovation promotion, institutional dialogue, cultural diplomacy and community engagement, RomLux will continue to serve as a key facilitator of Luxembourg–Romania cooperation and a long-term partner for its members and stakeholders.

The association remains committed to building bridges, creating opportunities and strengthening Europe through meaningful bilateral action.
